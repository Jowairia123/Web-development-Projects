Historical Perspective

The commission on Science and Technology for sustainable Development in the South (COMSATS) is an international organization.
It aims to reduce the ever-growing gap between the developed and developing world through useful applications of science and technology. The Thirld World Academy of Sciences (TWAS) initiated the proposal for the foundation of COMSATS under the leadership of Nobel Laureate Dr. Abdus Salam.
